Website Management Solutions

Part of the problem of websites, is their creation and upkeep, as well as the ongoing factors such as SEO, web hosting, web domains and more.

Manage My Web offers a solution that tackles all of these problems in one package. Our "Silver" & "Gold" plans will remove the most common problems of websites for their owners.
As a team of developers with knowledge and training in other areas of web development, our company will provide you with a turnkey solution that could potentially save you money.

Our service removes the need for web developers, software engineers, SEO agencies, paid hosting, and domain acquisition.

Plans Available:

Silver

Our silver plan is designed for those who want basic website coverage. This plan will provide customers with a custom built website that will provide further information on their business, and will largely target lead generation.
Examples of our silver plan websites include business and product landing pages which focus on providing visitors with further information of their company, as well as provide means of contact and lead generation.

Gold

Our gold plan is bespoke in nature. This means that we work closely with all of our gold customers to provide a level of service as if we worked within their organisation. This approach allows us to provide a platform and performance that is unmatched by agencies.

This plan is perfect for larger projects, such as online retail, news organisations and professional blogs. Our bespoke website solution will provide customers with custom development to allow for lead generation, payment processing, automated marketing, advanced search engine optimization, custom graphics, and more.

Due to the nature, and large scope of our gold plan. We would ask clients to engage with us for a free consultation to see how we can work together.
